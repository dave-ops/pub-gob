Common Command Listing

Overview of the most common commands. No command is case sensitive.

Command Use Aliases
look [target]

Obtain the description of something. If you do not give a target the room description is returned.

 l
score

Your character stats and current condition.

 sc, stats
inventory

Listing of items on your person.

 i
north, east, south, west, down, up

General movement commands.

 n, e, s, w, d, u
equipment

View worn equipment and open item slots. A worn item is still reflected in your inventory.

 eq
kill [target]

Begin combat with a target.

 k, adv, murder, attack
